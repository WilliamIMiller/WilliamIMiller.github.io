TGP (Group work)

Group Project || C# || Unity

Over 2, 3 weeks blocks with a group of 8 we built a dungeon crawler puzzle in Unity. As part of this project I worked on the dungeons, how each room connects, moved between by the player, the rooms communicating with enemies and traps so not to be active when the player is not in the room along with a variety of puzzle and trap elements.
